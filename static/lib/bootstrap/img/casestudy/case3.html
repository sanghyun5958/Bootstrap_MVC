UN Women’s iLEARN

• iLEARN is UN Women’s global mobile-learning platform for women entrepreneurs. Launched in 2015, it provides learning resources on the concepts of women’s economic empowerment as well as the basics of business such as finance, management, marketing, sales, human resources, networking, etc.

• Taking a story-telling-based training, iLEARN provides a platform for women entrepreneurs to tell their stories in their own words. Each lesson is delivered through stories of women entrepreneurs ‘behind the business plan’ including their motivation, decision, knowledge, skills, experiences, lessons learned which led to their business success.

What does it do?

iLEARN has currently six sections (each section consist of a series of women’s stories along with ‘questions to the reader’ and ‘summary of lessons’).
The core contents include: 

Sections
Contents
mySelf Women entrepreneurs share with you how they manage their company’s budget, revenue and expenses, and how they became credit worthy.
myMoney Women entrepreneurs share with you how they manage their company’s budget, revenue and expenses, and how they became credit worthy. Separate private and business finances to help your business grow.
myCompany Women entrepreneurs describe different business models and the key elements of business operations.
- Building a resilient business
- Assess the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and possible threats to your business
- Use networks of women entrepreneurs to reach out to customers in otherwise hard-to-reach areas
- Create and manage a profitable business to address a social problem.
myProduct Women entrepreneurs share key information about sales and marketing, and how to add value to products and services.
- Integrate culture, tradition and innovation into your business model
- Leverage your skills and experience to fill a gap in the market
- Get more customers and grow your business by adding value to your products
- Growing a business requires a unique and good quality product. 
myPeople Women entrepreneurs describe how they manage their employees and business partners, and expand their business networks.
- Expand your company by training and empowering others to deliver your business services.
- Motivate and train your employees and prepare them to help you grow your business.
 - Trust yourself and build credibility with employees and partners: it is key to your business success. 
myCourse MyCourses offers relevant learning opportunities for business women. The first course focuses on introduction to business development with sub-modules on the Business Model, Marketing, Sales and Customer Acquisition and Finance. Other courses, such as on negotiations, will be added soon.

Outcomes

• iLEARN is currently available in Arabic, Bangla, English, French, Hindi, Spanish, and Swahili. Through EmpowerWomen.org (UN Women’s online knowledge gateway), it is offered to the 170,000+ users and 6,000+ registered members using computers, tablets and smart phones.
• In partnership with Facebook’s internet.org initiative, iLEARN is expanding its availability to more women in developing countries with limited internet access. In South Africa, iLEARN became a part of Facebook’s internet.org application which is offered for free for everyone without connectivity cost. Recently, UN Women and Facebook hosted two “Boost Your Business” workshops training more than 400 women entrepreneurs in South Africa.
