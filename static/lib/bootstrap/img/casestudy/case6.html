Vodafone Turkey’s Women First Programme

Vodafone Women First is a programme launched by Vodafone Turkey which uses mobile services to provide women with education and skills training, expand access to job opportunities and help them improve their livelihoods.

What does it do?

• The Women First Advertisement Service, a key part of the programme, enables women entrepreneurs to sell their handicrafts and products on one of Turkey’s biggest online marketplaces, sahibinden.com using an easy-to-understand mobile application. 
• Through SMS messages, they can send information about their products to a third party organization, funded by Vodafone, which verifies the information and then posts an advert online on their behalf. To encourage more women to subscribe, the service also provides promotional offers and information services on topics such as child health and wellbeing.  

Outcomes:

• In the first nine months alone, Vodafone Turkey’s Women First Programme gained over 75,000 subscribers of which 15% were new customers for Vodafone. During that period, 4,700 adverts were placed, which were viewed by over 240,000 people and generated average sales of $51 per user.

• Muazzes Özer, a women entrepreneur in Turkey says:
“For years I have been interested in making handcraft arts. Producing and designing my work was time consuming and I had to spend most of my day travelling to neighbouring villages and cities to sell my artwork. Thanks to Women First Mobile Advertisement Service, I started to advertise my work through my mobile phone which triggered my sales. Joining the service allowed me to save time, money, run my business effectively and get access to a wider consumer group. Ultimately, my earnings increased approximately by 500%. ”

• The programme won two Communitas Awards; for Corporate Social Responsibility and Community Service.
